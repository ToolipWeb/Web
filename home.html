How we work

We strive to provide maximum comfort to our customers, which is why we want them to know from the start how we operate. We divide our projects into week long segments, each focusing on a different aspect.

Week 1

REQUIREMENTS

3 meetings

In these meetings we will establish the goals of the project together, formed by the client's requirements and our inputs

Week 2

DEVELOPMENT

2 meetings

Here we implement what was established in the previous week. Meetings here will have the goal of clarifying problems and informing on progress

Week 3

TESTING

2 meetings

In this week we imagine worst case scenarios and test our project on them to make sure it holds up. These meetings will be to inform on progress and patch things up

Week 4

IMPLEMENTATION

1 meeting

The final project will be ready and prepared for use. In the meeting, the client will be shown step by step how to use the product

* This structure may change depending on the project
